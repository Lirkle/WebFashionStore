About the Brand

History, philosophy and craftsmanship of Maison Margiela

Brand History

Founded in 1988 by Martin Margiela, Maison Margiela has become a symbol of deconstructivism in fashion. Each collection is an exploration of the boundaries between tradition and innovation.

From the very beginning, the brand was distinguished by an anonymous approach to design, where attention was focused on the clothing itself, not on the personality of the creator. This revolutionary vision changed the fashion industry.

Today, Maison Margiela continues the traditions of avant-garde design, remaining true to the principles of deconstruction and reimagining classical forms.

Philosophy

We believe in the power of anonymity and collective creativity. Each garment tells a story without drawing attention to its creator.

Our philosophy is based on deconstruction principles - we dismantle traditional forms and create something new, unexpected and inspiring.

Each collection is a dialogue between past and future, between tradition and innovation, between individuality and universality.

Craftsmanship

Every element is created with attention to detail, using traditional techniques and modern technologies.

Our craftsmen work with the highest quality materials, applying centuries-old tailoring traditions combined with innovative approaches.

Each product undergoes strict quality control to meet the brand's high standards and our customers' expectations.
